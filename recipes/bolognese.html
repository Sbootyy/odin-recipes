Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a delicious dish that is easy to make. With simple ingredients it is possible to create nuanced flavours that people of all age will enjoy

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 250g groundbeef
  • 1 package of sauce
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. boil heavily salted water
  2. cook groundbeef on medium-high temperature
  3. add pasta to boiling water
  4. create sauce in the beef pan, cook for 5mins
  5. add pasta to the sauce
